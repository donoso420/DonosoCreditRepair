FAQ

Clear answers before you enroll. Your exact service terms and disclosures are provided in writing before enrollment.

How long does credit repair take?

Most clients are active for 3 to 6 months. Initial file review is usually completed within 3 to 7 business days after onboarding documents are received. First dispute cycle typically starts within 7 to 14 days. Bureau and creditor responses often take about 30 to 45 days per cycle.

Can you guarantee deletions or score increases?

No. We do not guarantee deletions, score increases, approvals, or exact timelines. Results vary based on your credit profile, account history, and how bureaus or furnishers respond.

What documents do I need to start?

Most clients need a government-issued ID, proof of address, and current 3-bureau credit report access. If you have prior dispute letters or bureau responses, upload those during onboarding.

How often will I receive updates?

You receive regular progress updates during each cycle and a monthly status summary. Active Dispute Management and Rebuild + Coaching include priority support with a target response window of 24 business hours.

Do I still need to pay my existing bills and debts?

Yes. You should continue making payments on valid obligations. Credit repair support does not replace your payment responsibilities to lenders or creditors.

What is included in each plan?

Foundation Audit ($350) covers report review and action planning. Active Dispute Management ($500) adds monthly dispute workflow support and tracking. Rebuild + Coaching ($700) adds bi-weekly coaching and deeper rebuild strategy support.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Cancellation rights and instructions are included in your signed agreement and required disclosures before service begins. You can also submit a written request using our secure consultation form and include your full account details.
