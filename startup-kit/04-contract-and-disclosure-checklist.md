# Contract and Disclosure Checklist (Compliance Draft)

Use this with your attorney. Do not launch until every required item is validated for your state and sales model.

## Federal baseline (credit repair context)

- Written contract before services.
- Required disclosures delivered in required format.
- Clear statement of client's right to cancel (minimum statutory period).
- No prohibited representations in marketing/sales.
- Billing structure validated for CROA and telemarketing constraints where applicable.

## Contract checklist

- Legal business name and address (`Donoso Credit Repair, [BUSINESS ADDRESS]`)
- Client legal name and address
- Full description of services
- Timeline estimate language (non-guaranteed)
- Total cost and billing terms
- Performance expectations and client responsibilities
- Cancellation instructions
- Signature block and date
- Delivery method for notices

## Disclosure checklist

- Consumer rights statement (as required)
- No guarantee disclosure
- Negative item dispute limitations disclosure
- Cancellation rights notice with method and deadline framing
- Data handling/privacy disclosure
- Arbitration/class waiver language only if attorney-approved in your jurisdiction

## Marketing and sales checklist

- No "guaranteed score increase" language
- No "instant deletion" or "new identity/CPN" language
- Testimonials include disclaimer where needed
- Ads and landing pages mirror actual service terms
- Sales script matches contract terms

## Operations checklist

- Record retention policy
- Complaint handling process
- Refund and cancellation workflow
- Staff compliance training completed
- Audit trail for every client file

## Website legal checklist

- Terms of Service live
- Privacy Policy live
- Disclosure page live
- Cancellation policy live
- Contact and complaint submission path visible
