# Service Menu and Pricing (Draft)

Use this as your base offer sheet. Keep outcomes realistic and avoid guaranteed score claims.

## Positioning statement

`Donoso Credit Repair` helps clients identify and challenge potentially inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable credit reporting items while improving credit habits through structured coaching and accountability.

## Core service tiers

## What not to promise

- "We guarantee a 100-point increase."
- "We remove all negative items."
- "New credit identity / CPN tactics."

## Approved claims framework

- "We help you challenge potentially inaccurate reporting."
- "We help you build stronger credit behaviors."
- "Results vary based on each client's credit profile and response from bureaus/creditors."
