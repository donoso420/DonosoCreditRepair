# Client Intake Form (Draft)

Build this form in your CRM or form tool (Typeform, Jotform, HubSpot, etc.).

## Section A: Basic identity

- Legal first name
- Legal last name
- Date of birth
- SSN/ITIN (secure collection only)
- Email
- Phone
- Preferred contact method
- Current address
- Previous address (if less than 2 years current residency)

## Section B: Credit goals

- Primary goal:
- mortgage
- auto loan
- personal loan
- better rates
- general credit health
- Target timeline (`[DATE]`)
- Target score range (optional)

## Section C: Credit profile snapshot

- Known negative items (charge-offs, collections, late payments, repossessions, bankruptcy, etc.)
- Open revolving accounts count
- Installment accounts count
- Utilization estimate
- Current missed payments? (yes/no)
- Active disputes currently in process? (yes/no)

## Section D: Employment and income (optional, if relevant to coaching)

- Employment status
- Estimated monthly income range
- Estimated monthly debt obligations

## Section E: Supporting documents

- Government-issued ID
- Proof of address
- Credit monitoring access confirmation
- Recent 3-bureau credit report (if available)
- Any prior dispute letters/responses

## Section F: Authorizations and acknowledgments

- Acknowledgment of no guaranteed results
- Acknowledgment that client must continue paying valid debts
- Consent to communication method(s)
- Consent to electronic delivery of notices/documents
- Signature + date

## Section G: Onboarding logistics

- Best day/time for monthly review
- Preferred language
- Notes for your case manager

## Intake completion triggers

Mark onboarding as complete only when:

1. Signed agreement is on file.
2. Required disclosures delivered and acknowledged.
3. Cancellation notice process documented.
4. All required docs are uploaded.
5. Payment setup matches your compliance model.
