# Credit Repair Company Starter Kit

This kit gives you a practical launch foundation for a U.S.-based credit repair business.

## What's included

- `01-service-menu-and-pricing.md`: Service tiers, deliverables, and pricing framework.
- `02-website-sitemap-and-copy.md`: Website structure and draft copy.
- `03-client-intake-form.md`: Intake form fields for onboarding.
- `04-contract-and-disclosure-checklist.md`: Compliance checklist (CROA-focused).
- `05-30-day-launch-plan.md`: Day-by-day execution plan.

## Important legal note

This kit is an operational draft, not legal advice. Before launch, have a qualified attorney in your state review:

- service agreement
- disclosures
- payment timing/workflows
- marketing claims

## Quick start

1. Replace all `[PLACEHOLDER]` values.
2. Finalize pricing and billing timing with counsel.
3. Publish the website pages in `02-website-sitemap-and-copy.md`.
4. Build your onboarding form from `03-client-intake-form.md`.
5. Follow the launch sequence in `05-30-day-launch-plan.md`.
