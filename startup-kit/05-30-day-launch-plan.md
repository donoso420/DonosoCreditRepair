# 30-Day Launch Plan

This plan assumes you are starting from zero and want first clients quickly without skipping compliance.

## Week 1 (Days 1-7): Foundation

1. Form business entity and EIN.
2. Open business bank account and bookkeeping software.
3. Finalize brand name, domain, business email.
4. Pick core tools:
- website builder
- CRM
- e-sign tool
- payment processor
- secure file intake
5. Attorney review kickoff for agreement/disclosures.
6. Finalize service tiers and pricing draft.

Deliverables by Day 7:

- legal entity active
- draft offer sheet complete
- compliance counsel engaged

## Week 2 (Days 8-14): Website + Systems

1. Build pages from `02-website-sitemap-and-copy.md`.
2. Publish legal pages with reviewed language.
3. Build consultation booking flow.
4. Build intake form from `03-client-intake-form.md`.
5. Set up automated emails:
- lead confirmation
- consultation reminder
- onboarding next steps
6. Set up CRM pipeline stages:
- New lead
- Consult scheduled
- Consult completed
- Docs pending
- Active client
- Paused/canceled

Deliverables by Day 14:

- website live
- lead capture tested
- onboarding flow tested

## Week 3 (Days 15-21): Sales Engine

1. Build sales script aligned with contract terms.
2. Create consultation deck:
- process
- timeline expectations
- pricing
- compliance statements
3. Set up tracking dashboard:
- leads/week
- consult show rate
- close rate
- CAC
- churn/cancellation rate
4. Create 10 short-form educational posts.
5. Start local partner outreach list (realtors, loan officers, tax pros, auto professionals).

Deliverables by Day 21:

- repeatable consult process
- first inbound and partner leads

## Week 4 (Days 22-30): Launch + Optimization

1. Soft launch with personal network and partner referrals.
2. Run consultations and close first clients.
3. Review each step for friction:
- booking
- contract signing
- intake completion
- payment completion
4. Tighten follow-up automation for no-shows and undecided leads.
5. Publish 2 authority pieces:
- "How credit disputes actually work"
- "What credit repair can and cannot do"
6. Weekly compliance check on sales and marketing claims.

Deliverables by Day 30:

- first paying clients
- documented onboarding SOP
- KPI baseline for next 60 days

## Weekly KPI targets (starter)

- 20-30 new leads/week
- 8-12 consultations/week
- 20-35% close rate
- 80%+ onboarding completion

Adjust targets after first 4 weeks with real funnel data.
